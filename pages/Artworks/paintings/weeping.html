Name: THE WEEPING WOMAN

Artist: Pablo Picasso

Discription: The Weeping Woman is an oil on canvas painted by Pablo Picasso in France in 1937. Picasso was intrigued with the subject, and revisited the theme numerous times that year. This painting, created on 26 October 1937, was the most elaborate of the series. It has been in the collection of the Tate Modern in London since 1987, and is currently located at the Tate Modern, London.read more.

Dimensions:60 х 49 cm, 23 ⅝ х 19 ¼ inches
